Our Framework

Biotechnological research and development follows the design-build-test loop: design an experiment based on a hypothesis, build an experimental apparatus in the laboratory, and test your hypothesis so you can improve it with the experimental data. Every step of that loop is usually done manually, takes time and money, and can fail or even lead you astray, making your research and development take too long, cost too much, discover too little, or even fail outright. We consult you and deliver a custom design-build-test loop that automates these steps to spiral quickly, cheaply, and surely toward discovery. We are scientists who developed this framework to help other scientists: we will understand your problem and the work you do to solve it. Therefore, our approach follows the loop step-by-step. First, we automate the design step by applying breakthrough small-data machine learning software to develop a model from your real-world experimental datasets and optimize the experimental inputs for the outputs you want, saving you the time and trouble of doing so yourself and the risk that you might miss the pattern. Next, we automate the build and test steps with standard high-throughput experimental equipment that can asssemble many experimental samples simultaneously and repeatedly, with each sample being cheap and the cost of the equipment amortized over them. Third, we close the loop with newly commercially-available automatic cloud data management services that ingest the data from the high-throughput equipment and return it to the machine-learning software for another cycle.
