Be first!

In biotech, you have to be first. First to file a patent to protect your IP. First to get regulatory approval to sell your product. First to go to market to build a brand customers like and trust. First to scale production to grow market share and reach profitability. But first, you have to finish your research, and just a handful of setbacks requiring your scientists to rework their model can delay it by months. By cutting those weeks down to days with our framework, you can virtually eliminate that delay and be first.

Better Hypotheses Faster

Scientists derive hypotheses by developing a machine learning model from experimental data. Developing a model by hand takes weeks, but we use automated machine learning software guided by our scientific expertise to develop one in a day. Automated machine learning software is not limited by conventional wisdom or human intuition, so it can discover models even experts would never imagine. We apply our scientific expertise to configure the software to find an accurate model from which your scientists could derive a hypothesis.

Accelerated Research

Science is a trial-and-error cycle of developing a model from your data, deriving a hypothesis from your model, doing an experiment to test your hypothesis, and then updating or even replacing your model based on the results. Developing and updating by hand takes weeks that add up with every go-around, each of which might be another dead end. Our framework shortens the cycle to a day and makes each repetition likelier to be a step toward the truth.
