We apply our software framework to your project.

Manually fitting your model to the data from each new experiment and then tuning it to your liking is slow, laborious, and error-prone, but Frankentech has developed a new software-and-science framework to make it fast, easy, and accurate. First, we automatically test many variations of your model against your data on cloud computers, evolving them into a well-fitting one with machine learning, and then likewise optimize the model to do what you want. Bioinformatic Expertise

We automatically evolve your model through countless iterations and possibilities with cutting-edge algorithms, each generation closer than the last, until it fits your data.
