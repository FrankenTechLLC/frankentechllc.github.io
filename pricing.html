How we get paid

Time, milestone, and value based pricing.

Our pricing is designed to align our incentives with yours throughout a project. We start with a free consultation to discuss your problem, how we can help, and plan our cooperation. We then bill hourly for the initial consulting, where we learn the details of your project and detail the plan, because you would want us to attend as many meetings as you need for as long as you want. We break deliverables into milestones and charge for each one, so you only pay for what you get and we want to make it quickly. Finally, for especially large or ongoing projects, we offer value-based pricing so we make money when you make money.

Time

Our free initial consultation is 30 minutes, and a meeting with us both costs $100 per hour, pro-rated.

Milestone

We divide your deliverable into milestones based on its features, with a risk premium for features that would take longer to implement or involve new technology.

Value

We work with you to determine the monetary benefit you would gain from the improvement of your key performance indicators through our services. The larger and surer the benefit, the smaller the portion we charge.
