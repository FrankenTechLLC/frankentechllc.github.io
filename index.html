Research should be easy.

Rather than guessing different simulation inputs and hoping they work, we try many inputs on cloud computers and apply machine learning to their results to guide the search.

We start by simulating your biological system, from environment to metabolism. Then, we simultaneously run the simulation over a range of inputs on cloud computers, compare their results to your target, and seed another range with the inputs that yielded the best results. Repeating this loop until the best results are close enough reveals the inputs your simulation needs to do what you want, from secreting a rare enzyme to growing into a beating heart.

APES Workflow

How APES frames problem solving

Simulate

Evolve an expert guess of simulation conditions by trying variations simultaneously on the cloud and applying machine learning to their results.

Validate

Test the guess in the lab. If observation matches simulation, go to market; if not, you have learned something about your simulation.

Iterate

Improve your simulation with experimental results or new ideas from experts and try again to get better results.
