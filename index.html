Computational research should be easy.

Rather than guessing different simulation inputs and hoping they work, we try many inputs on cloud computers and apply machine learning to their results to find ones that do.

We start by simulating your biological system. Then, we simultaneously run the simulation over a range of inputs on cloud computers, compare their results to your target, and seed another range with the inputs that yielded the best results. Repeating this loop until the best results are close enough reveals the inputs your simulation needs to do what you want, from secreting a rare enzyme to growing into a beating heart.

Our Workflow

Automating discovery together

Simulate

We evolve your best guess of the inputs your simulation needs into a hypothesis by applying machine learning to cloud simulations.

Validate

You test our hypothesis in the lab. If observation matches simulation, go to market; if not, you have learned something about your simulation.

Iterate

Together, we improve your simulation with experimental results or new ideas from experts and try again to get better results.
