Bioinformatics should be easy.

Rather than tweaking your model and hoping it fits, we use automated machine learning on cloud computers to evolve it into one that does.

Fitting your model to the data from each new experiment is slow, laborious, and error-prone. Our new framework changes that: we automatically test many variations of your model against your data on cloud computers, evolve them into a well-fitting one with machine learning, and then likewise optimize the model for your purpose.
