Hypothesize at Hyperspeed

Our software-and-science consulting framework helps your scientists develop better hypotheses faster, letting them experiment more often and be likelier to succeed, so you can be first to file a patent, get to market, and scale up production.

Our framework automates the trial-and-error of research. First, we code your scientists' model of your system with our bioinformatic expertise. Next, we deploy that model alongside your data to the cloud, where our automated machine learning pipeline evolves it to fit and then optimize it. Finally, you experimentally test your newly evolved, optimized model and return the results so we can further improve it.
