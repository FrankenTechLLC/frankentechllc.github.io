Automatic Parallel Exploration of Systems

Our framework automates the trial-and-error of research. First, we code your scientists' model of your system with our bioinformatic expertise. Next, we deploy that model alongside your data to the cloud, where our automated machine learning pipeline evolves it to fit and then optimize it. Finally, you experimentally test your newly evolved, optimized model and return the results so we can further improve it.

Our automated machine learning on cloud computers evolves your model to fit your data. Then, generations of parallel runs optimize your model inputs for the outputs you want. Finally, you try our model and inputs in the lab. Scale if they work or iterate with us if not.

Evolve

Our automated machine learning on cloud computers evolves your model to fit your data. We consult with your researchers to configure our automated machine learning software with our bioinformatic expertise and your literature review, thesis, and experimental results. Our software then evolves this educated guess into one that fits the data by by considering generations of various models.

Optimize

Generations of parallel runs of your model optimize its inputs for the outputs you want. A model of a system works must be tuned for the behavior you want, from secreting life-saving enzymes to growing into a beating human heart, and our software does that just like it fits your model, trying one generation of varied inputs after another until the model output is what you desire.

Experiment

Try our model and inputs in the lab. Scale if they work or iterate with us if not. We deliver the fit and optimized model to you for testing. If you are immediately satisfied, you can immediately scale production and go to market; if not, tell us, and we will re-configure our automated machine learning software with your new results for another evolution, bringing you another step toward the truth.
