Simulation Development

We code your pen-and-paper model into a working biological simulation, help you do it yourself, or subcontract development of big models. If you have one already, we make it APES-ready.

APES Application

We work with you to develop a target to measure your simulation results against. Then, we deploy your simulation, target, and our APES software to the cloud, where we will discover the inputs your simulation needs to reach the target.

Expert Collaboration

We work with your scientific and technical staff to improve your simulation with their experimental results, expert knowledge, and literature reviews to better approach your target.
